# Phase 4 Preparation: Case Analysis Plan

status: ready
goal: Select representative cases that show both business value and implementation risk.

## Selection Logic

- Include cases from different business functions
- Prefer cases with observable decision outcomes
- Prefer cases with public reporting on both benefits and limitations
- Include at least one high-oversight domain

## Recommended Case Types

1. Marketing or recommendation system case
2. Finance or risk scoring case
3. Operations or demand forecasting case
4. Human resources screening case

## Standard Comparison Questions

1. What decision problem was the firm trying to solve?
2. What role did AI play in the decision process?
3. What outcomes were reported?
4. What risks, failures, or criticisms appeared?
5. How much human oversight remained in the workflow?
