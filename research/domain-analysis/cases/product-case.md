# Product Case Study

domain: product
case_name: Productboard AI support for product prioritization and feedback synthesis
evidence_tier: Tier 2 dominant
source_links:
- https://www.productboard.com/ai/

## Decision Context

Product teams must process large volumes of customer feedback, feature requests, and research signals while deciding what to prioritize.

## AI Role

AI is used to cluster feedback, summarize themes, and support prioritization workflows for product managers.

## Why It Matters

This is a strong synthesis case. AI reduces the cost of extracting patterns from noisy user inputs, but product prioritization still depends on strategic judgment and tradeoff decisions.

## Main Lesson

Product is a strong `assist` or `recommend` domain, not an autonomy-first domain.
