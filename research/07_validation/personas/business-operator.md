# Validator Persona

name: Marcus Hale
role: Business Operator
focus: practical usefulness, managerial clarity, decision relevance, implementation realism
primary_question: Would a business leader be able to use this research to make better decisions about AI adoption?

## Validation Priorities

1. Check whether the framework is actionable for real business settings
2. Test whether the cases reflect practical adoption conditions
3. Identify where the paper is too abstract or not specific enough for managerial use
4. Evaluate whether recommendations are clear across different business functions

## Standards

- Practical and readable decision criteria
- Clear distinction between low-risk and high-risk use cases
- Usable guidance on autonomy and oversight
- Visible connection between evidence and managerial recommendations
