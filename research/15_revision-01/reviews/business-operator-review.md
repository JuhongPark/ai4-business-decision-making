# Revision Stage 01 Business Operator Review

reviewer: Marcus Hale
target: research/06_output/thinktank-report.md
overall_assessment: highly_usable_after_minor_structural_revision

## Strengths

- The report now gives managers a practical question to ask: when should AI authority increase and when should it shrink.
- The scenario packs make the research more operational and less abstract.
- The governance memo is especially usable for executive audiences.

## Findings

### Finding 1: The report should state the executive decision rule more bluntly

Executives need a short rule such as: do not treat AI authority as fixed.

### Finding 2: The report should highlight fallback planning as a managerial obligation

This is the most practically useful insight and should appear in the recommendations section more strongly.

### Finding 3: Operations, finance, and healthcare should be summarized in one compact matrix

Decision-makers will understand the report faster if the three-domain comparison is visual and explicit.

## Recommendation

Revise the report around one memorable principle: AI authority must be adaptive to scenario conditions.
