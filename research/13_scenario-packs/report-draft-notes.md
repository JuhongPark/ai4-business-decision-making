# Report Draft Notes

status: completed
purpose: Convert the first scenario pack findings into report-ready implications.

## Operations Draft Implication

Operations should be presented as the clearest candidate for controlled automation, but only under stable and measurable conditions with explicit fallback mechanisms.

## Finance Draft Implication

Finance should be presented as a domain where AI can materially improve analytical support, but where scenario stress pushes the preferred mode back toward assist because fairness and model-risk costs rise under uncertainty.

## Healthcare Draft Implication

Healthcare should be presented as a high-value decision-support domain where recommendation support may be justified in routine or surge conditions, but safety-critical edge cases require clinician-led judgment.

## Cross-Domain Draft Implication

The report should argue that the correct unit of analysis is not only the domain. It is the domain-scenario-role combination.
