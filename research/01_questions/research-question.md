# Research Question

recommended_research_question: What factors determine whether AI-supported business decisions lead to better organizational performance?
question_status: refined in phase 1 scoping

## Supporting Questions

1. In which business functions is AI most commonly used for decision support?
2. What kinds of decisions benefit most from AI tools?
3. What organizational and technical factors influence the quality of AI-supported decisions?
4. What risks arise from the use of AI in business decision processes?
5. How should firms balance AI recommendations with human judgment?

## Working Argument

AI improves business decision-making most effectively when it augments human judgment, is supported by high-quality data, and operates under clear governance and accountability.

## Scope Anchor

This question will be examined at the firm level across strategy, marketing, finance, operations, and human resources, with AI treated as a decision-support capability rather than a fully autonomous decision maker.
