# Phase 12: Think-Tank Research Program

status: active
duration: 3 months
program_type: think-tank style applied research
title: AI in Business Decision-Making
subtitle: A Scenario-Based Study of Human-AI Collaboration Across High-Stakes and Operational Domains

## Program Objective

Develop a publishable think-tank style report that explains when organizations should use AI to assist, recommend, or automate business decisions across different domains and scenarios.

## Core Research Question

Under what conditions does AI improve decision quality across alternative business scenarios when it is used to assist, recommend, or automate decisions?

## Research Thesis

AI is not uniformly beneficial across business contexts. Its appropriate role depends on decision structure, downside risk, evidence quality, and the organization’s ability to govern the system responsibly.

## Program Workstreams

### Workstream 1: Conceptual Foundation

- define decision roles: assist, recommend, automate with guardrails
- define evidence tiers
- define evaluation metrics for quality, speed, explainability, and harm

### Workstream 2: Domain Comparison

- compare strategy, operations, finance, healthcare, investment, product, marketing, and market research
- identify governance-first and automation-friendly domains

### Workstream 3: Scenario Research

- build scenario packs for operations, finance, and healthcare
- compare human-only, AI assist, AI recommend, and AI automate modes
- test baseline, stress, and edge-case conditions

### Workstream 4: Case and Evidence Review

- maintain casebook
- classify claims by evidence strength
- distinguish company-reported from regulator-backed outcomes

### Workstream 5: Governance and Recommendations

- define restriction logic for high-stakes domains
- derive practical deployment guidance
- produce executive-facing and policy-facing outputs

## Planned Outputs

- long-form think-tank report
- executive summary
- scenario pack appendix
- domain comparison matrix
- governance recommendation memo
