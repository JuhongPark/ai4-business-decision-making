## Current Workflow

1. Define the research problem and scope
2. Review academic and industry literature
3. Compare high-stakes and lower-risk business cases
4. Analyze benefits, limitations, restrictions, and evidence quality
5. Build a practical framework for human-AI collaboration
6. Draft conclusions and recommendations
7. Refocus the study around high-stakes decision design
