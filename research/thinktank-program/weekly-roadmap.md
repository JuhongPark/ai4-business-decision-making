duration: 12 weeks

### Week 1

- finalize scope
- finalize main research question
- finalize report architecture
- confirm evidence-tier method

### Week 2

- confirm evaluation dimensions

- complete cross-domain comparison matrix

- draft conceptual framework section
- draft cross-domain findings section
- prepare scenario study design

- build healthcare scenario pack
- integrate safety, uncertainty, and triage logic

### Week 8

- compare scenario findings across all three domains
- extract autonomy recommendations and governance differences

### Week 9

### Week 10

- draft strategic recommendations for firms

### Week 11

### Week 12

- revise argumentation
- tighten evidence labels
- finalize publication-ready package
