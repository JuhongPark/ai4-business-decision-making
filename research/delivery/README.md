## What Belongs Here

- polished reports, summaries, diagrams, and briefings
- publication support tables and evidence registers
- freeze, checklist, claims, and submission packaging materials
- traceable validation passes that justify external-facing confidence

## Boundary Rule

- If a document explains how the work is run, it belongs in `operations/`.
- If a document is something a reader, reviewer, or submitter would consume, it belongs here.
- Treat `output/` and `submission-ready/` as the canonical external-facing entry points.
