# Phase 16: Domain Mapping

status: completed
purpose: Map each business domain into the category system.

## Domain Map

| Domain | Typical Structure | Typical Risk | Default Scenario Assumption | Default Mode | Why |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Strategy | unstructured | medium to high | baseline | assist | context-heavy and judgment-intensive |
| Operations | structured | low to medium | baseline | automate with guardrails | repeated and measurable decisions |
| Finance | semi-structured | high | baseline | assist or tightly governed recommend | fairness, compliance, and audit burden |
| Healthcare | semi-structured | very high | baseline | assist or tightly governed recommend | safety-critical with strong governance burden |
| Investment | semi-structured | high | baseline | assist or tightly governed recommend | investor-protection and disclosure burden |
| Product | semi-structured | medium | baseline | assist or recommend | synthesis-heavy with strategic tradeoffs |
| Marketing | structured to semi-structured | medium | baseline | recommend | strong feedback loops but meaningful external risk |
| Market Research | unstructured to semi-structured | medium | baseline | assist | interpretation and inference remain human-led |

## Important Note

This mapping provides defaults, not permanent rules. Scenario conditions can change the preferred mode within the same domain.
