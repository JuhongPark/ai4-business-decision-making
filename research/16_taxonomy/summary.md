# Phase 16 Summary

status: completed
goal: Turn the research into a reusable taxonomy for deciding when and how AI should be used.

## Main Result

The study now has a formal category system:

1. domain
2. decision structure
3. risk level
4. scenario condition
5. AI role
6. evidence strength

## What This Solves

This prevents the research from collapsing into vague case-by-case reasoning. Instead, it creates a structured way to decide:

- when AI should assist
- when AI can recommend
- when automation is acceptable
- when fallback is required

## Core Conclusion

AI authority should be assigned by category combination, not by generic enthusiasm and not by domain label alone.
