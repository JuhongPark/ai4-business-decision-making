# Framework Summary

title: Conditions for Effective AI-Assisted Business Decision-Making

## Core Claim

The value of AI in business decision-making depends less on model sophistication alone and more on the fit between decision structure, data readiness, oversight design, and governance.

## Summary Formula

better_ai_decision_outcomes: structured_decision + relevant_data + measurable_feedback + human_oversight + proportional_governance

## Practical Rule

The higher the stakes and the greater the potential external impact, the stronger the requirement for explainability, human review, and formal governance.

## Strategic Use

This framework should be used to:

1. evaluate where AI is appropriate
2. determine the right level of autonomy
3. identify governance needs before deployment
4. compare cases across business functions
