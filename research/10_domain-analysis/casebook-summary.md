# Phase 10 Casebook Summary

status: completed
goal: Provide one representative case study for each business field in scope.

## Casebook

1. strategy: Johor Corporation and Microsoft AI-enabled strategic decision support
2. operations: UPS ORION route optimization
3. finance: Upstart AI lending and underwriting
4. healthcare: FDA-governed AI-enabled medical device decision support
5. investment: SEC-framed robo-adviser and automated investment support
6. product: Productboard AI support for product prioritization
7. marketing: Netflix personalization and recommendation systems
8. market research: Qualtrics AI support for research synthesis

## Cross-Case Signal

- strategy, product, and market research are strongest as augmentation domains
- operations and marketing are the clearest candidates for controlled automation
- finance, healthcare, and investment are governance-first domains where risk and accountability dominate deployment design

## Main Use

This casebook should be used before any deeper domain-specific follow-up. It offers a broad comparative base rather than a final verdict on maturity or ROI in each field.
