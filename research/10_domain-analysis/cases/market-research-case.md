# Market Research Case Study

domain: market research
case_name: Qualtrics AI support for research synthesis and insight extraction
evidence_tier: Tier 2 dominant
source_links:
- https://www.qualtrics.com/artificial-intelligence/

## Decision Context

Research teams need to process large volumes of survey responses, qualitative feedback, and customer signals in order to generate insights for business decisions.

## AI Role

AI is used to summarize, cluster, and extract themes from qualitative and quantitative research inputs.

## Why It Matters

This case shows the speed advantage of AI in research operations, but also the risk of synthetic fluency masking weak inference or poor research design.

## Main Lesson

Market research is a strong `assist` domain. AI should help analysts work faster, not replace sampling logic, interpretation, or methodological judgment.
