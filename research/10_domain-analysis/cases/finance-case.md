## Decision Context

Lenders want to improve credit assessment, approval quality, and pricing while managing compliance and fairness exposure.

## AI Role

Upstart uses AI-supported underwriting and pricing models to inform consequential lending decisions.

## Why It Matters

This is a high-value, high-scrutiny case with primary regulatory documentation. It shows that AI in finance can create value, but governance burden rises with decision impact.

## Main Lesson

Finance should default to `assist` or tightly governed `recommend`. Strong model performance is not enough without strong oversight, monitoring, and accountability.
