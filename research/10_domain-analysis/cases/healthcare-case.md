# Healthcare Case Study

domain: healthcare
case_name: FDA-governed AI-enabled medical device decision support
evidence_tier: Tier 1 dominant
source_links:
- https://www.fda.gov/medical-devices/software-medical-device-samd/artificial-intelligence-software-medical-device

## Decision Context

Healthcare organizations increasingly use AI-enabled software for imaging, triage, and clinical support, but these tools operate in patient-safety-critical environments.

## AI Role

AI is used to support diagnostic, prioritization, and clinical workflow decisions through software as a medical device and related clinical support functions.

## Why It Matters

The FDA framing shows that healthcare AI must be evaluated through safety, effectiveness, transparency, and lifecycle governance rather than convenience alone.

## Main Lesson

Healthcare is a governance-first domain. AI should usually remain `assist` or tightly governed `recommend`, not broad automation.
