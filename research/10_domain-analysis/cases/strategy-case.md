# Strategy Case Study

domain: strategy
case_name: Johor Corporation and Microsoft AI-enabled strategic decision support
evidence_tier: Tier 2 dominant
source_links:
- https://news.microsoft.com/source/asia/features/ai/jcorp-microsoft-copilot/

## Decision Context

Johor Corporation needed faster access to enterprise-wide knowledge and more coherent information flows across business units in order to support coordination and strategic decision-making.

## AI Role

Microsoft Copilot and related AI tools were used to improve information retrieval, summarization, and knowledge access across the organization.

## Why It Matters

This is a strategy-support case rather than an automation case. The AI value comes from reducing search and synthesis costs, not from replacing executive judgment.

## Main Lesson

Strategy is a strong augmentation domain. AI can widen situational awareness and accelerate synthesis, but final strategic choices remain highly contextual and management-led.
