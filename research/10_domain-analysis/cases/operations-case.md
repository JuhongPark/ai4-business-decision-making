domain: operations
case_name: UPS ORION route optimization
evidence_tier: Tier 2 dominant

## Decision Context

UPS needed to optimize route sequencing and service efficiency across a very large delivery network.

## AI Role

ORION and related navigation tools support route sequencing, navigation, and repeated operational decisions in the field.

## Why It Matters

This is one of the clearest lower-risk automation cases because the decisions are frequent, measurable, and operationally constrained.

## Main Lesson

Operations is a strong candidate for `automate with guardrails`, but only when data quality, exception handling, and system-level constraints are well managed.
