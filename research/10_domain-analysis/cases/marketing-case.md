domain: marketing

evidence_tier: Tier 2 dominant

## Decision Context

Netflix must help users discover relevant content efficiently across a large and dynamic catalog.

## AI Role

Recommendation systems support ranking, discovery, and personalized presentation decisions.

## Why It Matters

Marketing and personalization are among the strongest commercial AI use cases because decisions are repeated, feedback-rich, and directly tied to user behavior.

## Main Lesson

Marketing can justify `recommend` and, in bounded cases, `automate with guardrails`, but firms still need to watch for privacy, bias, and short-term optimization traps.
