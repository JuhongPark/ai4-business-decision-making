## Decision Context

Investment firms use automated advisory tools to scale portfolio recommendations and investor support.

## AI Role

Automated investing tools and robo-advisers use algorithmic logic to recommend portfolio choices, risk alignment, and account actions.

## Why It Matters

This domain combines strong data intensity with strong investor-protection concerns. Automation can widen access, but it also raises disclosure, suitability, and conflict-of-interest issues.

## Main Lesson

Investment is not an automation-first domain. AI is best positioned as `assist` or tightly governed `recommend` under clear disclosure and accountability rules.
