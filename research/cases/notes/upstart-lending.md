## Business Problem

Lenders want to improve credit access, pricing accuracy, and approval quality while managing default risk and regulatory exposure.

## AI Application

Upstart uses AI-based underwriting and pricing models that combine traditional and non-traditional variables to support lending decisions for bank partners.

## Decision Supported

The system supports loan approval, underwriting, and pricing decisions.

## Reported Outcomes

CFPB materials described the platform as using alternative data to expand access to credit and required regular reporting to monitor consumer risk. Upstart investor materials also report transaction growth and position the platform as improving lending efficiency, but those company-reported outcomes should be interpreted separately from regulator-backed evidence.

## Risks or Limitations

This case shows that high-impact decisions require regulatory scrutiny. The CFPB termination order highlights that model changes can alter risk exposure and fairness implications, making governance a central issue rather than an afterthought.

## Relevance to Framework

This is the strongest high-oversight case in the project because it combines direct regulatory evidence with company-reported business claims. It shows that business value and governance pressure rise together when AI influences consequential financial decisions.
